Overview

Branding for the client "Noblish." Noblish is an English education company which targets on intermediate/Low-advanced level of English leaners in Korean with needs of high-business manner/dimension. Based on their marketing strategy to teach business English, the client wanted to give a high-end quality of a brand image which aligns with their curriculum.

Skill set

Illustrator, Photoshop

Duration

3 days
(December 4th ~ December 6th, 2017)

Works




Emblem is a simplified shape of N derived from the brand name ‘Noblish’. Brand color is golden yellow to present the luxurious image. The font was selected as Niagara Solid, rather than using San Serif fonts in order to avoid a feeling of boring, uninteresting and dull.

Process

Drawing: Started playing around with the letter "N" and decided to visualize it into parallelograms.





Variation: Came up with 3 different versions -- parallelograms, letter with N and E, combined form of triangles and a parallelogram which resembles the letter "N."






Name card: After confirming the logo design, I made name cards based on that.
