Maxim Project

The second project of Core Interaction Studio at Parsons communication design course is called Maxim. By definition, maxims are a short, pithy statement expressing a general truth or rule of conduct. For this project, we collected a set of phrases from an existing source. We considered the content of maxims, what are they about and where are they coming from, how does the content change based on the hierarchy of our design and the way we're breaking it up.

Designed by Aileen

For this maxim project, Aileen adopted abstract visualization of Chanel perfumes with the use of circles, without showing a literal description about perfumes. Circles with pastel color variation create subtle mood for the entire website. Viewers can jump right into experiencing fragrances of Chanel perfume through hover effect.
